About Us

10 Years Of Experience in this area

Indesign was established in 2010 with a mission to transform empty spaces into extraordinary livable areas. Our hand picked team, which consists of highly qualified engineers and designers with more than 15 years of experience, always strives to insure that you get the best service and design possible. Our process starts from our planning room where the designs are visualized in photorealistic 3d and shared with our clients for approval. When everything is green lit the transformation process begins as we start working on location.
